Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

At Scan Aushadhi, privacy is built-in. We don't want your data; we just want to help you understand medicines.

Disclaimer: This app is informational and NOT for medical advice.

1. Zero Data Logging

  • No Accounts: No signup or login is required.
  • No PII: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Temporary Photos: Scanned images are processed and deleted instantly.

2. Essential Permissions

We only ask for Camera and Storage access to analyze your medicine labels. We cannot access your other private files.

3. AI Processing

We use secure AI APIs to read medicine text. This process is anonymous. No personal info is ever sent to third parties.

4. Security

Your session is encrypted. Since we don't have a database of users, there's nothing for hackers to steal.
